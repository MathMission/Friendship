Sunway Pre-U Friendship Run

A community running and walking event designed to bring together Sunway Pre-U students, alumni, and friends in a shared experience of movement and connection.

📸 Follow updates on Instagram: @sunwayfriendshiprun

Overview

The Friendship Run is a non-competitive community event open to all fitness levels. Participants may choose to walk, run-walk, or run the course at their own pace.
